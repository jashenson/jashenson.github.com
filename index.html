Jared A. Shenson

Biography

Jared A. Shenson is a fourth-year M.D. candidate at Vanderbilt University School of Medicine and an aspiring otolaryngologist. His research interests lie at the intersection of medicine, design, and technology. Currently he is evaluating the use of patient-provider electronic messaging and investigating approaches to improve the care of patients with otolaryngic illness. He has also designed, built, and assessed novel educational technologies based on the pioneering Quick Medical Reference project. Mr. Shenson also serves as the co-president and co-founder of the Student Technology Committee at the School of Medicine, a group dedicated to advancing the state of educational technology. He graduated with a B.S. with distinction from Yale University in 2012, and has previously founded and consulted for companies developing innovative software for the education and non-profit communities.
